---

## The Problem: Exposition Dumps

**Definition:** AI explicitly states what characters think/feel instead of letting readers infer from actions, dialogue, and context.

**Why LLMs Do This:**
- Risk aversion: Explaining is "safer" than trusting reader inference
- Training data bias: Academic/journalistic writing favors explicit statements
- Lack of "trust" in context: AI doesn't assume readers remember previous paragraphs

**Impact on Light Novels:**
- Kills dramatic tension
- Insults reader intelligence
- Breaks immersion with meta-commentary
- Turns character moments into psychology textbooks

---

## Core Principle: SHOW, DON'T TELL

**Bad (Telling):**
> "She was angry because he forgot their promise. This made her feel betrayed and hurt."

**Good (Showing):**
> "She turned away, fists clenched. 'You forgot.'"
