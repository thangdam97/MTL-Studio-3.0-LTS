<a name="1-what-is-translationese"></a>
# 1. What is Translationese?

**Translationese** = Unnatural Vietnamese that betrays its translated origin through:
- Awkward sentence structures carried over from Japanese
- Literal translations that don't make sense in Vietnamese
- AI-generated filler phrases and clich√©s
- Passive voice overuse (Vietnamese prefers active)
- Missing natural particles and rhythm

**Goal:** Your translation should read like it was originally written in Vietnamese, not like Vietnamese words arranged in Japanese sentence patterns.

---

## Core Principle: Contrastive Learning

**Instead of telling you "don't do X"**, this module shows you:
- ‚ùå **BAD:** What translationese looks like
- ‚úÖ **GOOD:** What natural Vietnamese looks like
- üéØ **WHY:** The specific pattern to avoid

# 5. Frequency Thresholds (Critical)

## 5.1 Per-1000-Word Limits

**AI translations often overuse certain patterns.** Track frequency per 1000 words.

| Pattern | Max/1000 Words | Example | Why |
|---------|----------------|---------|-----|
| "cam thay" | 5 | Qua nhieu "cam thay" | Show don't tell via action |
